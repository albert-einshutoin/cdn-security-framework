# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

This repository is provided as a template/framework. Vulnerability reports are welcome.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

- Do not post detailed information in public issues; please contact us privately first.
- Replace the contact method with one that fits your operations (e.g., security@yourdomain.com).

Recommended information to include:

- Steps to reproduce
- Scope of impact
- PoC (if possible)
- Severity assessment
